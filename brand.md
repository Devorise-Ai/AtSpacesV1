# at Spaces | Brand Guidelines 2025

## 1. Introduction
These guidelines ensure a clear and consistent implementation of the **at Spaces** brand and visual identity.

### Purpose of This Document
*   **Inspiration:** Helps you understand the brand and explore its creative potential.
*   **Information:** Provides full details to maintain a strong, unified identity.
*   **Instruction:** Outlines the rules for applying the brand correctly.
*   **Examples:** Demonstrates proper application via stationery and presentation templates.

---

## 2. Brand Identity
### Brand Archetype (Dual Model)

#### 60% — The Explorer
*   **Promise:** Freedom to find your own way.
*   **Goal:** Live authentically through discovery.
*   **Motivation:** Freedom and self-realization.
*   **Strategy:** Break boundaries and inspire curiosity.
*   **Positioning:** A pioneer in tech-driven space booking and a facilitator of discovery.

#### 40% — The Caregiver
*   **Promise:** Care and support.
*   **Goal:** Help people feel safe and supported.
*   **Motivation:** Service and belonging.
*   **Strategy:** Offer comfort and reliable service.
*   **Positioning:** Trustworthy, accessible, and committed to user comfort.

### Brand Positioning Summary
**at Spaces** is a tech-driven platform connecting individuals and businesses with flexible, on-demand access to meeting rooms, training halls, and event spaces across Saudi Arabia. We enable vendors to list spaces while users book and pay only for the hours they need.

**Tagline:** *Any time, any where, by the Hour!*

---

## 3. Tone of Voice
The **at Spaces** voice should always feel:
*   **Friendly:** Warm, approachable, and welcoming.
*   **Empowering:** Encouraging exploration and control.
*   **Empathetic:** Understanding diverse user needs.
*   **Reliable:** Professional and clear.
*   **Helpful:** Guiding users with proactive support.

> **Example**
> *   **Instead of:** "Browse through our spaces and book online."
> *   **Use:** "Explore nearby spaces and book the perfect one, anytime, anywhere, by the hour."

## 4. Logo System
The logo represents rooms, digital innovation, and modern booking technology. It consists of a recognizable symbol and a modern digital typeface.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Note:** The logo assets are located in the `media` folder.

### Logo Variations
*   Full-color primary logo
*   Monochromatic version
*   Symbol-only version (for social avatars, watermarks, or limited space)

### Usage Rules
*   **Clear Space:** Maintain minimum padding around the logo.
*   **Minimum Size:** 0.375 inches (Print) | 48 pixels (Web).
*   **Prohibitions:** Do not stretch, compress, recolor, rotate, or add effects (shadows/outlines).

## 6. Typography
### English: TT Lakes Neue Trl Exp
*   **Style:** Modern, Geometric, Highly Legible.
*   **Hierarchy:**
    *   **Black:** Large headlines (>28pt)
    *   **Bold:** Standard headlines (<28pt)
    *   **Semibold:** Titles
    *   **Regular:** Body text
    *   **Light:** Supporting info

### Arabic: DIN Next LT Arabic
*   **Style:** Clean, Professional, Balanced.
*   **Hierarchy:** Black (Headlines), Bold (Headers), Regular (Body).

### System Fallback
*   **Arial:** Use for emails, MS Office, or when brand fonts are unavailable.

---

## 7. Graphics & Visuals
### Illumination Graphic
A key visual element that reinforces brand presence and adds a tech-forward tone.
*   **Rule:** Use sparingly; should not exceed 60% of the visual field. Always use approved artwork.

### Icons & Infographics
*   **Style:** Solid brand colors, clean outlines, minimal design.
*   **Rule:** Do not create custom or use external icons. Simplify info, don't just decorate.

---

## 8. Applications
All brand applications—including business cards, email signatures, stationery, and presentations—must strictly follow the color, typography, and logo placement standards defined above.
