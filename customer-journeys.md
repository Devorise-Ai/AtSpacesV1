# AtSpaces Customer Journeys

**Product:** AtSpaces – Find Your Perfect Workspace  
**Core Promise:** Trust, Transparency, and Convenience

---

## 1️⃣ Customer Journey: Workspace Seeker (Primary User)

### Phase 1: Discovery
**Goal:** Find a suitable workspace quickly and confidently.

*   **Entry Points:**
    *   Direct website visit
    *   Social media ads
    *   Google search
    *   Referral
*   **User Actions:**
    *   Enter location
    *   Select space type (meeting room, workshop space, etc.)
    *   Choose date & time
    *   Browse search results
    *   Apply filters (Verified Only, amenities, accessibility)
*   **User Emotions:** Curious, Hopeful, Slight uncertainty
*   **Pain Points:** Information overload, Uncertainty about availability, Difficulty finding exact matches.
*   **Platform Experience:** Fast search results (< 2s), Clear pricing, Verified badges, Smart filters.
*   **Strategic Outcome:** User feels confident and in control.

### Phase 2: Evaluation
**Goal:** Compare options and select the best value.

*   **User Actions:**
    *   Open space details page
    *   Browse gallery images
    *   Review amenities grid
    *   Compare pricing bundles (Hourly, Half-Day, Full-Day)
    *   Evaluate included services (WiFi, catering, projector)
*   **User Emotions:** Analytical, Evaluating, Increasing confidence
*   **Pain Points:** Understanding pricing differences, Comparing multiple listings efficiently.
*   **Platform Experience:** Standardized amenities grid, Transparent bundle structure, Visual gallery.
*   **Strategic Outcome:** User moves from uncertainty to confident decision-making.

### Phase 3: Checkout
**Goal:** Complete booking securely and quickly.

#### Step 1 – Review
*   User reviews booking summary.
*   Sees price breakdown (e.g., 2 Hours × 15 JOD = 30 JOD).
*   Views cancellation policy (Free until 48 hours prior).
*   **Emotion:** Focused

#### Step 2 – Guest Details
*   Auto-filled info for logged-in users.
*   Optional notes for special requests.
*   **Emotion:** Efficient

#### Step 3 – Payment
*   Select Credit Card or E-Wallet.
*   Secure transaction processing.
*   **Emotion:** Slight tension → Relief

#### Step 4 – Confirmation
*   Instant success message.
*   Options: Get Directions, Add to Calendar.
*   **Emotion:** Relieved and satisfied

**Pain Points Eliminated:** No hidden fees, No complicated forms, No delayed confirmation.  
**Strategic Outcome:** User completes booking smoothly with high trust.

### Phase 4: Post-Booking
**Goal:** Prepare for the visit.

*   **User Actions:** Add to calendar, Get directions, Review confirmation email.
*   **User Emotions:** Satisfied, Excited, Prepared
*   **Platform Experience:** Immediate confirmation email, Calendar integration, Map directions.
*   **Strategic Outcome:** User feels supported even after checkout.

---

## 2️⃣ Secondary Journey: Returning Customer

*   **Motivation:** Previously positive experience, Familiar with process, Wants speed.
*   **Differences:** Uses saved preferences, Less browsing, Faster checkout.
*   **Emotional State:** Confident, Decisive, Efficient
*   **Strategic Impact:** Higher conversion rate, Lower friction, Higher lifetime value (LTV).

---

## 3️⃣ Vendor-Enabled Journey (Indirect but Critical)

*   **Vendor Contribution:** Provide accurate space details, Upload high-quality images, Maintain availability.
*   **Impact on User Journey:** Enables trust, Reduces cancellations, Improves satisfaction.

| Phase | Emotional State Shift |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Discovery** | Curious → Focused |
| **Evaluation** | Evaluating → Confident |
| **Checkout** | Focused → Relieved |
| **Post-Booking** | Satisfied → Excited |

---

## Strategic Pillars Reinforced Throughout Journey

1.  **Trust Through Verification:** Verified badges reduce anxiety.
2.  **Transparency Through Pricing:** Clear breakdown eliminates hesitation.
3.  **Convenience Through Flow:** Auto-fill and instant confirmation reduce friction.

> [!NOTE]
> **AtSpaces Assistant:** A chat bot interface is available as a floating window throughout the entire journey to provide instant assistance, answer FAQs, and resolve user uncertainties in real-time.

## Final Strategic Summary

The AtSpaces customer journey is designed to:
*   Reduce cognitive overload
*   Eliminate pricing confusion
*   Build trust early
*   Minimize checkout friction
*   Extend value beyond booking

**Result:** A high-conversion, trust-driven, user-centered workspace booking platform.
